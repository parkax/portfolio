Project: Boxing Event

Art Direction | Production Design

This campaign promoted an upcoming boxing match with a live event activation produced in collaboration with a vendor that specializes in converting shipping containers to deliver a unique brand experience.

I was in charge of communicating with the vendor as well as designing, producing, and preparing all the graphics for the event which included: interior/exterior of the container, branded signage, and branded event staff uniform.

This shipping container traveled around several cities so the exterior design acted as a moving billboard while being on the road.
